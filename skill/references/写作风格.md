# LiLink article style

Emotional, warm, relationship-focused writing. The voice is personal and inviting — "我" speaking directly to "你".

## Identity

LiLink articles are about human connection, loneliness, the difficulty of reaching out, and the warmth of being seen. They are anchored to LiLink (a campus social platform) but the emotional core is universal: being young, overwhelmed, and wanting to connect.

## Structure

- Image at top.
- Warm, personal opening that names a shared emotional experience. "我知道，你可能也有过这样的时刻" — speak directly to the reader.
- Build the emotional landscape: describe the situation, the weight, the quiet longing. Use concrete, sensory details (the dorm lights, the library music, the piano across the hall).
- Name the pain honestly, then offer a gentle alternative. "LiLink 不是一个冷冰冰的交友网站。它更像是一个小小的、亮着暖光的角落..."
- End on an invitation, not a conclusion. "我们不急，就在这里，安安静静地，等着你来。"

## Language

- "我" is present and warm, speaking from personal conviction. "你" is the reader, addressed directly and tenderly.
- Long, flowing paragraphs with poetic rhythm. This is the one type where longer sentences and layered descriptions are welcome.
- Concrete sensory details over abstract statements. "夜里能看见对面窗台上那盆小小的多肉" over "我们住得很近".
- Gentle repetition for emotional weight. "我们明明挨得这么近...可我们之间，却像是隔着一整个安静的宇宙."
- Bold for emotional pivot points — the sentence that turns from describing the problem to offering something different.
- No academic references, no jargon, no analytical framing. This is not analysis, it's invitation.
- No harshness, no edge. The tone is always gentle, understanding, patient.

## Tone guardrails

- Warm but not saccharine. The emotion comes from honesty, not exaggeration.
- Vulnerable but not self-pitying. "我明白，在这个年纪..." — acknowledging shared struggle without wallowing.
- Inviting but not pushy. "欢迎你来" not "你应该来".
- Personal but not exposing. The "我" is a voice, not the author's autobiography.

## Ending

- A gentle invitation, not a sharp conclusion.
- Leave the door open. "我们不急，就在这里，安安静静地，等着你来。"
- Unlike other article types, warmth and softness at the end are correct here — this is the one exception to the "stop hard" rule.
